Jian Ma

I am an Assistant Researcher (Postdoc) in Prof. Kun Li group at Tianjin University. My research interests include egocentric video understanding, 3D human reconstruction.

Email  /  Google Scholar  /  Github

Educations and Working Experiences

I completed my Ph.D. journey at University of Bristol under the supervision of Prof. Dima Damen and I achieved my master degree in computer science at the College of Intelligence and Computing at Tian University from 2016 - 2019, supervised by Prof. Kun Li. Additionally, I did internships at Baidu and OPPO Research Institute, under the supervision of Dr. Yandong Guo.
